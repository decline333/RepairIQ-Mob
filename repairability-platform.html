Reference — Difficulty Scale

The 8 Repairability Grades

1
Trivial
No tools needed. Anyone can do it in minutes with zero prior knowledge.
Filter change, bulb swap, belt clip
2
Easy
Basic screwdrivers only. Straightforward access, no disassembly risk.
Cover removal, descaling, drain cleaning
3
Simple
Common tools, low risk. A calm first-time repairer can succeed with the manual.
Pump seal, door latch, heating element
4
Moderate
Multiple steps, some disassembly. Patience and the manual are essential.
Coffee boiler, washing drum bearing
5
Intermediate
Technical skills required. Risk of additional damage if steps are skipped.
Laptop motherboard, motor rewind
6
Advanced
Specialist tools and strong technical understanding needed. Remote support recommended.
Inverter drive, conveyor gearbox
7
Expert
High complexity, electrical or safety risk. Professional-grade support mandatory.
PLC module, servo controller, HV PSU
8
Specialist Only
Requires certified technician or factory tools. DIY strongly discouraged.
High-voltage systems, sealed compressors
